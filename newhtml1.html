About Me

25 years old, I'm a self-taught designer, full-time web enthusiast and part-time volunteer.
I have a BSc in Applied Electronics and I study human behaviour independently. I'm pretty much fascinated about how the human mind works and that's why my goal is to create smart, intuitive designs that sell.

Below you'll find a short description of how I like to "tackle" projects, if you want to find out more about me you can view/download my CV or get in touch.

Imagine

Every project starts with an idea. Your precious. And you want others to know about it, now and forever...and for that you need a plan. I can help you plan, I can help you answer the Five Ws, find the strengths and weaknesses, opportunities and threats that surround your idea.

We can work together to define your goal and choose a solution that takes you there.

This first step is about:

  • Vision - Defining your aspirations, your business goals for the future
  • Mission - What is it that you're doing now to achieve your goals
  • Market Research - What is your target market, and how can your idea help them
  • Strategy - What are we going to do to advertise your idea
  • Timeline - An overview of all the steps, deadlines, milestones

Design

Your idea needs a shape, a visual representation of its core values. It's not just about the aesthetics, it's more about transmitting the right message. I like to design conversion oriented materials; websites, leaflets, brochures that sell your products or services.

Here we talk about:

  • Layout - The structure of the final product; pages, sections, containers...
  • User experience - Let's make the user's life as easy as possible
  • Graphics - Color combinations, styles, icons, typography.
  • Images - The biggest cliche of them all: an image is worth a thousand words
  • Feedback - Communication ensures a great result. Simple as that :)

Build

The stage where your idea finally comes alive. And it's crucial to be built on a solid structure. Clean, semantic code that is up to date with the latest developments in web-design, including user friendly content management systems that allow you to easily update your website yourself.

We move on to:

  • Code - Front-end technologies, Content Management Systems, functionality
  • Content - Populating your website with content
  • SEO - Search Engine Optimization. Becoming friends with Google
  • Advertising - Getting noticed. Online and offline marketing.
